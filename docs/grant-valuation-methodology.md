# Grant Valuation Methodology

How we calculate the annual in-kind value of each grant we help nonprofits unlock. This is the behind-the-scenes reference for the figures shown on the public For Nonprofits page (`/nonprofits.html`).

Each figure is what the nonprofit would otherwise pay at standard market rates for the same software or services. It is not cash transferred.

## Steps

1. **Count what the grant unlocks.** For each approved grant we identify the entitlement: number of free seats, storage allowance, monthly ad spend cap, or other in-kind benefit.

2. **Multiply by the retail rate, over 12 months.** We use the publicly advertised market price the nonprofit would otherwise pay, multiplied across all seats (or units) and over 12 months.

3. **Convert USD to CAD where needed.** USD-denominated grants are converted to Canadian dollars at the Bank of Canada annual average exchange rate for the grant's approval year. For round-number programs (Google Workspace, Google Ad Grants) we treat USD figures 1:1 as CAD.

4. **Recount on each approval.** Figures update only when a new grant is approved, renewed, or revoked. We do not double-count multi-year approvals.

## How each grant is valued

| Program | Calculation | Annual value |
|---|---|---|
| Canva for Nonprofits | 50 seats × CA$19/month × 12 | CA$11,400 / year |
| Microsoft for Nonprofits | 300 seats × US$6/month × 12, converted | ≈ CA$30,000 / year |
| Google Workspace for Nonprofits | ~1,000 seats × US$14/month × 12 | US$168,000 / year |
| Google Ad Grants | US$10,000/month × 12 | US$120,000 / year |

These are per-grant values. The totals shown on the public page include multiple grants per program across the nonprofits we work with.
