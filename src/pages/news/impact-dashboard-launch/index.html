Oasis of Change Launches Public Impact Dashboard to Strengthen Transparency in Tree Planting

New platform offers supporters, partners, and the public a clearer view into tree-planting progress, impact metrics, and organizational accountability.

VANCOUVER, British Columbia — March 18, 2026

Oasis of Change, Inc. today announced the launch of its new public impact dashboard, a custom-built platform designed to make the organization’s tree-planting work more transparent, accessible, and easy to understand.

Available at impact.oasisofchange.com, the dashboard provides supporters, partners, and community members with a centralized view of planting progress and related impact data. The platform was developed to turn environmental reporting into a more visual and publicly accessible experience.

The dashboard highlights key metrics such as total trees planted, partner-supported planting, geographic footprint, and other measurable indicators connected to Oasis of Change’s broader mission. By consolidating this information into one purpose-built platform, the organization aims to strengthen public trust and make it easier for stakeholders to follow progress over time.

“We wanted to build a platform that makes our impact easier to understand, easier to share, and more accountable to the people who support our mission.”

— Oasis of Change team

The launch reflects Oasis of Change’s ongoing commitment to digital sustainability and mission-driven technology. Rather than relying on fragmented reports across multiple tools, the organization invested in a platform designed specifically for clarity, transparency, and long-term public accessibility.

The dashboard also supports a broader shift in how nonprofits communicate impact. By making information easier to navigate and interpret, Oasis of Change hopes to help set a stronger standard for transparent storytelling and measurable public reporting.

In addition to improving public visibility into planting outcomes, the dashboard demonstrates how custom digital infrastructure can support nonprofit accountability, stakeholder engagement, and mission alignment.

Members of the public, partners, and media are invited to explore the new platform at impact.oasisofchange.com.

About Oasis of Change, Inc.

Oasis of Change, Inc. is a Vancouver-based nonprofit focused on digital sustainability, low-carbon web development, and measurable environmental impact. Through its work, the organization develops technology and digital infrastructure that support communities while advancing transparency, tree planting, and public engagement around sustainability.
