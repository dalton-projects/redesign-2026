Grants we help nonprofits unlock

Google Workspace for Nonprofits

Free Google Workspace for eligible nonprofits, a nonprofit-specific tier that includes 100 TB of pooled storage, mail, Drive, Meet, Docs, and admin controls under your own domain.2

Google Ad Grants

Up to US$10,000 per month in in-kind search advertising for eligible nonprofits, used to drive awareness, donations, and volunteer sign-ups.3

Canva for Nonprofits

Free Canva Pro features for teams of up to 50 users at eligible nonprofits. Includes brand kits, premium templates, photos, fonts, and team collaboration.4

Microsoft for Nonprofits

Microsoft 365 Business Premium grants and discounts, plus Azure credits for eligible nonprofits.5

TechSoup

A nonprofit-validation gateway to discounted and donated software from dozens of vendors, with admin fees only on most items.6

Atlassian for Nonprofits

75% discount on Jira, Confluence, and other Atlassian Cloud products for eligible registered charities.7

Claude for Nonprofits

Discounted access to Claude, Anthropic's AI assistant, for qualifying nonprofits. Useful for writing, research, accessibility, and operations.8

Methodology

How we calculate annual value

Each figure on this page is what the nonprofit would otherwise pay at standard market rates for the same software or services. It is not cash transferred.

Count what the grant unlocks

For each approved grant we identify the entitlement: number of free seats, storage allowance, monthly ad spend cap, or other in-kind benefit.

Multiply by the retail rate, over 12 months

We use the publicly advertised market price the nonprofit would otherwise pay, multiplied across all seats (or units) and over 12 months. For Canva for Nonprofits, that is 50 seats at about CA$19 per seat per month, which works out to 50 × $19 × 12 = CA$11,400 per year.

Convert USD to CAD where needed

USD-denominated grants are converted to Canadian dollars at the Bank of Canada annual average exchange rate for the grant's approval year.9

Recount on each approval

Figures update only when a new grant is approved, renewed, or revoked. We do not double-count multi-year approvals. Each approved grant contributes one annual-value figure per year of validity.
