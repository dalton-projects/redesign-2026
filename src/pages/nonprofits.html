Who this is for

Grants we help nonprofits unlock

We help nonprofits apply, qualify, and get set up across the platforms they already use.

Google Workspace for Nonprofits

Free Google Workspace for up to 2,000 users with 100 TB of pooled storage, custom domain email, Gemini AI, and NotebookLM.

Google Ad Grants

Up to US$10,000 per month of in-kind Google Search advertising credit for eligible nonprofits.

Canva for Nonprofits

One free team of up to 50 seats with all Canva Pro premium features and Canva Teams collaboration tools.

Microsoft for Nonprofits

Up to 300 free Microsoft 365 Business Basic licenses, plus 75% off Business Premium and other Microsoft cloud products.

TechSoup

Validates nonprofit status across 236 countries to unlock donated and discounted software from 90+ partners.

Atlassian for Nonprofits

100% off Teamwork Collection (Jira, Confluence, Loom) for up to 25 users, then 75% off Atlassian Cloud above that.

Claude for Nonprofits

Up to 75% off Claude Team for eligible nonprofits, with built-in integrations for Blackbaud, Candid, and Benevity.
